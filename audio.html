Audio Detection

Deceptive audio content, such as deepfake voice recordings or synthetic speech generated by AI, poses a significant challenge for authenticity verification and deception detection. Deepfake audio can be created by manipulating recordings of human speech, altering the speaker's words, intonation, or emotional tone to produce false or misleading messages. These artificially generated audio clips can be used to impersonate individuals, fabricate statements, or manipulate public discourse.
